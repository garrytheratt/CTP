men do cry.

This interactive video and website aims to raise awareness of depression amongst men, by visualising the mental and physical effects that the disease carries. Traditional social norms demand that men are tough and independent, this paradigm is long outdated.

We aim to break down this stigma that often prevents men from seeking help, through a deeper and wider understanding.

the abstract animation depicts just six effects that men commonly experience when suffering from depression, including: insomnia, anxiety, loneliness, panic and numbness.

THe accompanying audio portrays a man's admission of having mental health issues and his accompanying experiences, built from various accounts and detailed research.

Please note that the effects and symptoms covered in the interactive video are by no means definitive. Depression can affect sufferers in many different ways, only a few are covered in this feature. The manner inwhich the symptoms manifest can also differ from the artistic portrayals shown.
